Cycling Network and Bicycle Parking in Toronto

The blue lines on the map represent Toronto's extensive cycling network, providing safe and dedicated bike lanes for commuters and recreational riders alike. The pink points highlight the numerous bicycle parking racks scattered throughout the city, making it easier for cyclists to securely park their bikes.

Toronto's growing cycling infrastructure encourages eco-friendly transportation and promotes a healthier lifestyle. Consider choosing your bike over a car for your next trip—it’s good for you and the planet!
